Oxymoron

After thorough research, I found out "product manager" would be the best fit for me because product management requires not only managemnt skill-sets, but also design sense as well as passion for new technology. While I was working at Deloitte, I did self-study for Python (mostly during late night) as well as GRE for further pursuing a master degree in computer science. Altough it is not a easy transition from business/accounting to computer science, but I believed that having experience in both consulting combined with trainings in CS would be uniqe asset to become a product manager.
